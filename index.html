About

Luke Yuen is an incoming Master of Science candidate in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University. He is currently completing his undergraduate degree in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Engineering Design at Rice University. Luke has a strong interest in rapid prototyping, hands-on fabrication, and the development of innovative mechanical systems. His work blends technical precision with creative problem-solving, reflecting a commitment to building thoughtful, functional designs across a range of engineering applications.
