The Ideas Lab

At the MD Anderson IDEAS Lab, an innovation hub focused on surgical and patient care challenges, I collaborated with Krystal Kimiagaree under the direction of Dr. Justin Bird and Mrs. Savannah Esteve Morreale. Our work centered on solving retraction-related discomfort in orthopedic oncology surgeries by designing more ergonomic, surgeon-friendly tool interfaces. This experience deepened my understanding of clinical needs and demonstrated how thoughtful mechanical design can improve both surgeon performance and patient outcomes.
