VacuTrac

Novel Integrated Tissue Retraction and Suction Device for Spinal Surgery; Patent Pending

We aim to create a one-handed instrument that allows surgeons to retract muscle and suction blood simultaneously thereby reducing the surgeon's physical and mental fatigue, the number of personnel required to perform the procedure, and the time under anesthesia for elderly patients.

VacuTrac is a handheld suction device that enables surgeons to control three tools with two hands

Surgeons require multiple tools to achieve exposure and suction simultaneously—complicating procedures and requiring additional hands in the OR.

We engineered a handheld device that integrates controlled suction with a stable, ergonomic grip—freeing one hand without compromising performance.

By simplifying tool transitions and improving intraoperative efficiency, VacuTrac offers a scalable solution to support solo or minimally assisted procedures.

Patent Pending.

A provisional patent has been filed for this design, recognizing its originality and potential for clinical impact. This status marks an exciting step toward future development and commercialization.
